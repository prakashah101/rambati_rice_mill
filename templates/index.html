About Us

Quality You Can Trust, Freshness You Can Taste

Rambati Rice Mill has been serving Nepali households and local businesses for over a decade. We take pride in delivering the freshest, purest grain products — milled with care and packed with hygiene. From premium basmati rice to freshly ground wheat flour, every product is a promise of quality.

As a local Nepal business, we deeply understand our community's needs. That's why we offer home delivery, bulk orders for shops, and the convenience of cash on delivery — making quality grain accessible to every household.
